Our History

Unravel Ramen started with a simple idea, food should be made with care, not just for taste but for the people who eat it. From the lively streets of India to the heart of Singapore, we’ve always believed in one thing: quality. Quality in our ingredients, our process, and the way we serve our customers. Growing up, food was never just food. It was warmth, comfort, and a way to bring people together. That’s what we wanted Unravel to be. Every bowl we serve is made with fresh, locally sourced ingredients, slow-cooked broths, and noodles that are crafted with real intention. No shortcuts, no nonsense—just honest, good food. Because at the end of the day, Unravel isn’t just a ramen place. It’s a feeling. A reminder of home, no matter where you are. And that’s what we’ll always stand for.
